FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Velocity Motors

  1. Do you only sell new cars? No, we sell both new and certified pre-owned vehicles. All pre-owned cars go through a thorough inspection to ensure quality and reliability.
  2. Can I apply for vehicle financing online? Yes. Our website includes an online finance application form. Our finance team will review your application and get in touch with personalised options.
  3. Do you accept trade-ins? Absolutely! Bring in your current vehicle for an assessment and we’ll offer you a fair trade-in value that can be used toward your next purchase.
  4. How do I book a test drive? You can book a test drive online through our booking system or call our sales team to schedule a convenient time.
  5. Do you provide warranties on vehicles? Yes. All new vehicles come with manufacturer warranties, and our certified pre-owned vehicles include extended warranty options.
  6. Where is your dealership located? We are based in Pretoria, Gauteng. You can find our exact location and directions on the Contact Us page.
  7. Do you offer after-sales servicing? Yes. Our Service Centre provides routine maintenance, repairs, and genuine parts to keep your vehicle in peak condition.
  8. What payment methods do you accepts? We accept EFTs, debit/credit cards, and approved financing options through our partner banks.
  9. How often should I service my cars? We recommend servicing your vehicle every 12 months or 15,000 km, whichever comes first. Our service team can provide a tailored schedule.
  10. How can I reach customer support? You can contact our support team via phone, email, or through the website’s contact form. Our team is ready to assist during business hours.
